About Mjolnir Security

Mjolnir Security is a 100% Canadian-owned, pure-play cybersecurity firm with over 580 DFIR engagements completed. We hold SOC 2 Type 2 certification and specialize in operational technology and industrial control system security, helping critical infrastructure operators defend against nation-state threats and sophisticated adversaries.

VULNOT is our flagship training platform — born from lessons learned across hundreds of real-world engagements. A fully containerized OT lab environment providing hands-on experience with real industrial protocols, attack techniques, and defensive tooling, published to give back to the security community.
